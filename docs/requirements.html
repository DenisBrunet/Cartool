Technical Requirements

Cartool is designed to run exclusively on Windows. To ensure optimal performance, your system should meet the following requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or higher
  • Memory: At least 8GB (more is recommended for larger datasets)
  • Processor: A CPU such as an Intel i7 with more than 8 cores
  • Graphics: An OpenGL-accelerated graphics card, such as an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 40XX or newer
  • Display: A monitor with a large screen size for enhanced usability
  • Mouse: A 3-button mouse (the middle button is frequently used in Cartool)

 

For Mac Users:

Cartool has been reported to work well under the Parallels VM on macOS. However, some adjustments to the OpenGL settings may be required for optimal functionality.

Publications Citation

When using Cartool to process, manipulate, or display data in your research, it is mandatory to cite the software in any resulting publications. Additionally, the substantial public funding that supports the development of this software must be properly acknowledged.

We kindly request you include the following acknowledgment in your manuscript:

In the Acknowledgments Section:

The Cartool software (github.com/DenisBrunet/Cartool) was developed by Denis Brunet, University of Geneva, Switzerland, and is supported by the Center for Biomedical Imaging (CIBM) of Geneva and Lausanne.

In the Materials and Methods Section:

The analysis was conducted using the Cartool software by Denis Brunet (github.com/DenisBrunet/Cartool).

 

Your support in acknowledging this work is greatly appreciated. Thank you!
